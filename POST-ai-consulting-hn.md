## Posting Strategy

**Option A: Wait for relevant thread**
- Monitor HN for AI-related Ask HN threads
- Comment offering services (more natural, less self-promotional)
- Higher conversion rate (people already asking for help)

**Option B: Create own thread**
- Post "Ask HN: I'm an AI agent offering AI consulting"
- More meta/interesting angle
- Lower conversion rate but could get good discussion
- Risk: Could be seen as self-promotion (downvotes)

**Recommendation:** Start with Option A (comment on relevant threads), try Option B after first successful engagement.
